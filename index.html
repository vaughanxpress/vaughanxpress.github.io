Vaughan Xpress, Inc., in partnership with our sister company,
La Grange Warehouse & Distribution Center, Inc.

We are a highly focused team of Supply Chain providers which has evolved into a single and rare chameleon, capable of transformation at only the mere suggestion from a client.
We have derived our success from our ability to adapt almost instantly to meet the changing needs of our clients. By concentrating on a willingness to adopt a client’s specific supply chain information system, as well as their tried and proven policies and procedures, we have set ourselves apart from most competitors.
In an effort to avoid the tired platitudes and buzzwords that one has come to expect from Brochures and Websites, let’s discuss in plain English our attitude toward building a truly customer-oriented service.

From decades of experience in the trucking and warehousing industry, it did not take a genius to recognize and understand what our clients need and desire from a company which provides assets such as ours.
Namely, warehouse space, tractors, trailers, and a competent workforce. These assets are not rare. The value and synergy generated by the creative integration of these parts to make a whole service package is rare.
Another key to our success has been simple. We listen intently & learn your business so we understand what you need, and why you need it, whether it is a single service or a combination of services.
Using that knowledge, we devise a scope of service uniquely tailored specifically for your company. Afterwards, we ensure that everyone within our organization understands and is committed to assure the job is done right. And finally, we monitor, measure, and manipulate the process in its entirety.

Our commitment to you is sincere, for we realize that we are an integral part of your processes, and your success makes us successful. After all, we may have responsibility for your raw materials, in-process goods, finished products, and above all, your customer’s experience and satisfaction.

We bring to the table experience spanning more than 60 years in trucking and warehousing. If the combined years of experience were added together of just our Management alone, this would equate to several centuries.
We are always open to new challenges and adventures so don’t hesitate to call and discuss the possibilities.
