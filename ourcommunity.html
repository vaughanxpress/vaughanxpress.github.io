Local
We at Vaughan Xpress, Inc. believe these things are central and deserve our support:

  • Our People
  • Our town and community
  • Our schools
  • Our churches
  • Our soldiers
  • Our government
  • Family.

Accordingly, we consider it a privilege and joy to join local efforts, for example, to be a Partner in Education with our school system, and to provide flat beds and tractors to help with local parades, just to name two. 

Global
Looking after our greater environment is a sacred trust.

  • Think Green - Vaughan Xpress, Inc. is an EPA certified Smartway partner, because we are devoted to using technology to minimize the use of a valuable resource and to reduce the abuse of emissions and their effect on our environment.
  • C-T PAT – Vaughan Xpress, Inc. is a certified Customs Trade Partner Against Terrorism. We support our government’s efforts to preserve and protect.
  • Sustainability – Doing our best to sustain our company and, more importantly, our community and environment is only common sense. Further, we owe future generations the effort of leaving this planet even cleaner than we found it.
