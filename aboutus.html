Vaughan Xpress, Inc.
is a general transportation company with a specific attitude, which we express very directly. We take care of our customers. And we take care of our customers with pride in who we are and with friendly eagerness toward pleasing those we serve.

From our base in LaGrange, Georgia, about an hour southwest from Atlanta, we provide a wide array of services to move your freight locally, regionally, nationally, and throughout Canada. These services emphasize truck load, less than truckload, van and flat bed, and include others you can see when you access our services tab.  
 
Our commitment to our customers is very real, for we realize that we are an integral part of your processes. After all, we touch your raw materials, in-process goods, and finished products.

We understand that we often represent you as we communicate and deliver directly to your customers and their agents. 

So we bring to your service our more than 58 years of transportation experience, our three generations of founding family currently helping to manage your logistics, our office of qualified professionals, our well-maintained equipment, and a fleet of experienced, professional drivers.
